Hi! I am Giulio 👋🏻

I am a Ph.D. student in Energetics at Politecnico di Torino, deeply engaged in the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and materials science 🔬🖥️. My research revolves around applying AI techniques to discover and optimize advanced materials for energy applications, with a particular focus on perovskites for photovoltaics — pioneering materials that hold the promise of revolutionizing solar energy ☀️.

My journey began in energy engineering, but along the way, I developed a profound passion for materials science and the elegance of mathematical models. This passion drives my work today, where I merge computational intelligence with scientific curiosity to contribute to a more sustainable energy future.

Beyond the lab and algorithms, I am a romantic at heart, finding joy in the arts. I cherish the power of music 🎵, both as a listener and a musician, lose myself in the pages of novels that explore the depths of human experience 📖, and find inspiration in the beauty of visual art, particularly paintings 🖌️. This blend of science and creativity fuels my perspective and keeps me inspired in everything I do.

Thank you for visiting — let’s connect and share ideas!🫱🏻‍🫲🏾🚀
